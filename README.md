# Design of a Pickup Truck-Mounted Crane for Small Load Handling

## Project Overview

This project focuses on the design of a compact pickup truck-mounted crane intended for lifting and handling small loads in industrial, construction, and maintenance applications. The design emphasizes structural strength, operational stability, and ease of deployment while maintaining a lightweight and compact configuration.

The project includes CAD modelling, load calculations, structural analysis, engineering drawings, and design optimization for safe lifting operations.

---

## Problem Statement

Manual handling of heavy materials poses safety risks and reduces operational efficiency. Existing mobile cranes are often expensive and oversized for small-scale lifting applications. A compact truck-mounted crane provides a practical and economical solution for handling moderate loads.

---

## Objectives

- Design a compact truck-mounted lifting crane.
- Ensure structural safety under design loads.
- Optimize boom geometry and load distribution.
- Improve operational stability and lifting efficiency.
- Validate the design through engineering calculations.
